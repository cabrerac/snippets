### Exponentiated Quadratic Covariance {data-transition="none"}

$$k(\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{x}^\prime) 
= \alpha \exp\left(-\frac{\left\Vert \mathbf{x} - \mathbf{x}^\prime\right\Vert^2_2}{2\ell^2}\right)$$
